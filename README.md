### 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm **Palakurthi Venkatesh Goud**, a **Data Analyst** who loves turning messy numbers into clear stories. I'm early in my journey (1 month in!) but I've been building fast — from dashboards to full-stack AI apps.

- 🔍 I dig into data with **SQL, Python, and Power BI** to find the "why" behind the "what"
- 🤖 I'm exploring the world of **AI Agents, RAG, and LLMs** — building smarter tools, not just prettier charts
- 🌐 I build things end-to-end — from a **Pandas notebook** to a **React/Next.js** front end
- 🚀 Always learning, always shipping — check out my [portfolio](https://palakurthivenkateshgoud.github.io/my-portfolio/)
